About RR Foundation

We are committed to helping families recover, rebuild, and regain hope after fire disasters across the United States.

Our Story

The RR Foundation was created to provide immediate support for families who lose their homes to fire. When disaster strikes, most families have less than 5 minutes to escape leaving everything behind. Our organization steps in to provide emergency assistance, essential supplies, shelter support, and long-term recovery guidance.

What began with a small group of community members responding to a local fire has grown into a dedicated nonprofit serving families across the United States.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, immediate, and long-term support to individuals and families affected by fire disasters.
