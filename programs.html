Emergency Relief

The first hours after a fire are overwhelming. Our emergency relief program focuses on getting families what they need as quickly as possible ‚Äî support, supplies, and someone to walk them through the next steps.

Emergency Care Kits

We provide care kits with clothing, hygiene items, basic food, and other essentials tailored to each family‚Äôs situation.

On-the-Ground Support

Our team and partners meet families at safe locations to help with information, referrals, and coordination during the first 24‚Äì72 hours.

Limited Financial Assistance

When funding allows, we offer small emergency grants to help with transportation, lodging, or other urgent needs.

Shelter & Housing Support

After a fire, finding a safe place to sleep becomes the first priority. Our shelter and housing support program helps families bridge the gap between crisis and stability.

Emergency Shelter Referrals

We connect families with shelters, hotels, and community-based options so they have a safe place to stay in the short term.

Housing Navigation

We assist with exploring rental options, support programs, and other housing resources that can help families move forward.

Community Partnerships

By working with other nonprofits, faith communities, and local agencies, we increase the housing support available to survivors.

Recovery & Rebuilding Support

Recovery from a fire isn‚Äôt over in a week. Our recovery program focuses on the weeks and months after a fire, helping families rebuild their lives step by step.

Case Support

We help families understand available resources, make plans, and stay connected to support as they move through recovery.

Documents & Insurance Guidance

We provide guidance on replacing key documents and navigating insurance processes where applicable.

Emotional & Community Support

We connect families to counseling, support groups, and community resources that address the emotional impact of fire trauma.

Fire Safety & Community Education

We believe in reducing risk before disaster happens. Our fire safety education efforts give families and communities tools to prepare and prevent future fires.

Workshops & Trainings

We partner with local fire departments and community centers to host sessions on fire prevention, home safety, and evacuation planning.

Family Safety Resources

We provide checklists, printable plans, and simple guides to help families create and practice fire escape plans.

Outreach in High-Risk Areas

We focus education efforts in neighborhoods and regions at higher risk of residential fires to maximize impact.
